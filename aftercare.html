Tattoo Aftercare Instructions

Proper aftercare helps your tattoo heal beautifully and keeps your artwork looking its best.

First 24 Hours

✔ Leave your bandage on for the amount of time recommended by your artist.

✔ Wash your hands before touching your tattoo.

✔ Gently wash the tattoo with warm water and fragrance-free soap.

✔ Pat dry with a clean paper towel. Do not rub.

Healing Process

✔ Apply a thin layer of recommended aftercare lotion.

✔ Moisturize 2–3 times daily.

✔ Do not scratch or pick at peeling skin.

✔ Wear loose clothing around the tattoo.

Avoid

❌ Swimming pools

❌ Hot tubs

❌ Lakes or oceans

❌ Direct sunlight

❌ Excessive sweating during healing

Healing Time

Most tattoos heal in approximately 2–4 weeks. Continue moisturizing even after healing to keep your tattoo vibrant.
