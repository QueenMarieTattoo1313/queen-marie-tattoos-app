Tattoo Aftercare

Follow these steps to keep your tattoo looking beautiful while it heals.

First 24 Hours

  • Leave your bandage on for the amount of time recommended by your artist.
  • Wash your hands thoroughly before touching your tattoo.
  • Wash gently using warm water and fragrance-free soap.
  • Pat the tattoo dry with a clean paper towel. Do not rub.

Healing Process

  • Apply a thin layer of the recommended tattoo aftercare lotion.
  • Moisturize your tattoo 2–3 times each day.
  • Never scratch or pick at peeling skin.
  • Wear loose clothing to avoid rubbing the tattoo.
  • Drink plenty of water to help your skin heal.

Things To Avoid

  • Do not swim in pools, lakes, rivers, or hot tubs while your tattoo is healing.
  • Avoid direct sunlight on your new tattoo.
  • Do not soak your tattoo in a bathtub.
  • Avoid heavy workouts that cause excessive sweating for the first few days.
  • Never pick or peel scabs.

Healing Timeline

Days 1–3

Tenderness, redness, and slight swelling are normal.

Days 4–14

Your tattoo may itch and begin peeling. Continue moisturizing and do not scratch.

Weeks 2–4

Most tattoos finish the initial healing process. Continue protecting your tattoo from the sun.

Long-Term Tattoo Care

  • Apply sunscreen whenever your tattoo will be exposed to sunlight.
  • Keep your skin moisturized to maintain vibrant color.
  • Stay hydrated to keep your skin healthy.
  • Contact Queen Marie Tattoos if you notice signs of infection or have healing concerns.
