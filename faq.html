Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most common questions about appointments, deposits, tattoos, aftercare, and more.

Do I Need An Appointment?

Appointments are recommended to guarantee your preferred date and time. Walk-ins may be accepted depending on availability.

Is A Deposit Required?

Yes. A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment. Your deposit will be applied toward the total cost of your tattoo.

Can I Bring My Own Design?

Absolutely! Bring your ideas, reference photos, or artwork. Queen Marie can recreate, customize, or design a unique tattoo just for you.

Do You Offer Custom Tattoos?

Yes! Every custom tattoo is designed to match your vision, style, and placement for a one-of-a-kind piece.

Do You Do Cover-Up Tattoos?

Yes! Cover-up tattoos are available. Please send clear photos of your existing tattoo during the booking process so Queen Marie can determine the best design options.

How Much Do Tattoos Cost?

Tattoo pricing depends on the size, placement, detail, color, and time required. A quote will be provided during your consultation or after reviewing your design.

How Long Will My Appointment Take?

Appointment times vary based on the tattoo design and size. Smaller tattoos may take less than an hour, while larger custom pieces may require multiple sessions.

Can I Bring Someone With Me?

One guest may accompany you if space allows. To help maintain a comfortable and professional environment, additional guests may be asked to wait outside.

How Should I Prepare For My Appointment?

Please eat a good meal before your appointment, stay hydrated, get plenty of rest, and avoid alcohol or drugs for at least 24 hours before your session. Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the area being tattooed.

Do I Need Identification?

Yes. A valid government-issued photo ID is required for every appointment. No exceptions.

What Payment Methods Do You Accept?

Cash is always accepted. Other payment options may be available. Deposits can be paid securely through the Pay Deposit page in this app.

How Do I Reschedule My Appointment?

If you need to reschedule, please contact Queen Marie as soon as possible. Advance notice helps us accommodate your new appointment and other clients.

Still Have Questions?

We're here to help! If you couldn't find the answer you were looking for, contact Queen Marie Tattoos directly.
